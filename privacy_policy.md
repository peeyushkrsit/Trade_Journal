# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** [Date]

## 1. Information We Collect

TradeJournal collects the following information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, authentication provider (Google/Email)
- **Trading Data**: Trade entries, screenshots, notes, analysis results, and performance metrics
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the application (for improving user experience)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and improve our services
- Generate AI-powered trade analysis
- Send you important updates about your account
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## 3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform). We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure authentication
- Firestore security rules that restrict access to your own data only

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your trading data is private and only accessible to you.

**Third-Party Services:**
- **Firebase/Google Cloud**: Hosting and data storage
- **OpenAI or Google Gemini**: For AI analysis (your trade data is sent to these services for analysis only)

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View all your data at any time through the app
- **Delete**: Delete your account and all associated data from Settings
- **Export**: Export your trades in CSV format
- **Correction**: Update your profile information

## 6. Data Retention

Your data is retained until you delete your account. When you delete your account:

- All trades are permanently deleted
- All screenshots are permanently deleted
- Your user account is removed
- This action cannot be undone

## 7. Security

We take security seriously:

- All data is encrypted in transit
- Firestore security rules prevent unauthorized access
- API keys are stored securely as environment variables
- Regular security audits and updates

## 8. Children's Privacy

TradeJournal is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

## 10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app settings or create an issue in the repository.

## 11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other applicable data protection laws
